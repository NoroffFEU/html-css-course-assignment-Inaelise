About us

Rainy Days is a norwegian clothing brand located in Bergen. With the experience of nordic climate and weather we have designed and developed jackets fit for any activity in any weather.

We are dedicated to providing you with top-notch affordable jackets. Whether you're embarking on a rugged hiking adventure, hitting the slopes, or simply braving the elements in your daily life, our collection of jackets is designed to keep you warm, dry, and comfortable.
